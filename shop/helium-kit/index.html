Helium DIY Kit

Helium is a high-quality precision buffered multiple and precision adder and you can build your very own with this DIY kit!

This kit is a beginner level kit. You don't need any prior soldering experience to put it together, and it typically takes less than an hour to finish.

Check out the build guide to learn more.
