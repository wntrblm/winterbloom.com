Big Honking Button DIY Kit

Big Honking Button is a simple sampler in a very silly package and you can build your very own with this DIY kit!

This kit is a perfect for beginners- it's our easiest kit and it only requires soldering 6 through hole components! Even if you're new to soldering you should be able to get through the build in about an hour.
