Micronova bundle

The tiny but mighty Eurorack power supply with all the bells and whistles

Micronova is a miniature power supply with a big soul. It can provide up to 18W of power for your modules and multiple units can be easily daisy chained to power larger systems.

This bundle includes:

  • an assembled Micronova
  • an assembled Micronova busboard
  • a Micronova front entry kit

That's a whole $15 USD less than the items purchased separately.

Please note that the front entry requires assembly. It's a delightfully easy kit, but make sure you know what you're getting into!

A suitable AC-DC power adapter is required. We have adapters available for domestic customers. International customers please take a look at the manual for guidance and recommendations.

Check out the manual to get to know Micronova and all of the delightful ways you can integrate it into your case.
