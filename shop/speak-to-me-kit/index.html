Speak to Me DIY kit

Speak to Me is a rock solid high-quality headphone amplifier with a delightfully lo-fi speaker and you can build your very own with this DIY kit!

This kit is a beginner level kit. You should feel comfortable soldering through-hole components and wires before tackling this project. This kit is similar but slightly more involved than our Big Honking Button kit.
